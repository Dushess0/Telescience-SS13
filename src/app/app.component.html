About telescience

In Space Station 13, telescience is a part of RnD, which purpose is teleporting to derelicts (abandoned labs, space shuttles) and exploring them to find disks with important data or rare equipment. But everyone use it to steal Captain's spare ID or take weapons from weaponry. Remember you will be fired (banned) if you do this! (of course if you are a traitor there is no limits)

Ground control to major Tom

There are two parts of equipment: Telescience console and Telepad . Console used to enter values and open portal and telepad is spawning this portal. Console have next parametres: Bearing, Elevation, Power, Sector. They are defining a point (x,y,z) in space. Wait but this is 2D game, how it's possible it have 3 dimensions? Well, Z stands for sector or layer, there are 6 sectors, 1 and 5 are empty space, 2 is sector of station, 3 and 4 used for derelicts and 6 for mining outpost. You can view your position by using Global Positioning System (GPS), Wher round starts you need to link telepad to console, open maintenance panel of telepad with screwdriver , get data with multitool, close panel, upload data from multitool to console. Console needs at least one bluespace crystal to open the portal. Usually there are plenty of them in telescience lab. Take all of them and insert into console.

Liftoff

Bluespace crystals need to be calibrated first. After each callibration crystals change errors, you can open portal 10-20 times before next calibration. You need to find errors to open portal in exact point. There are two approaches to find errors: Power and Elevation. open wormhole, measure output location using GPS, change one of those parametres (power or elevation) on console then open next wormhole, measure second location. Enter data into calculator and now you have errors, now you can access every location.

Telescience is very dangerous job!

  • Take a weapon! Abandoned labs or stations have a lot of dangers: Syndicate soldiers, hive bots, combat drones, unknown biological forms,laser turrets, CLOWNS. You can take kinetic rifle from Research outpost. Use it to propel yourself in space, it has unlimited ammo and can kill somebody when used wise.
  • Take tools, you need them to make your way to the disk. Crowbar and screwdriver are essential minimum.
  • Take GPS and set tag as your name, it make easier to find your dead body
  • Notify somebody about your intentions, maybe they don't forgot about you
  • Telescience equipment consumes a lot of energy, let the engieneers configure engine and wait until APC will be fully charged.
